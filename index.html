What You Need To Know

Are you an African Creative Entrepreneur?

Colours from Africa is a crowdfunding fellowship by Thundafund designed to identify, train and mentor 40+ innovative, African Creative Entrepreneurs, in a series of expert-led Creative business workshops to crowdfund, create & showcase their product.

Showcasing African Innovation

The goal of this initiative is to help African Entrepreneurs, innovating in the creative industries, nurture and reposition their existing ventures as globally competitive businesses that showcase African ideas, values, systems, philosophies, ideologies, techniques etc.

Fundraise Effectively

The initiative seeks to empower businesses rooted in indigenous African ideology, with skills in brand building, brand story-telling, fundraising and product innovation and at the end, help their founders successfully fundraise Kshs 500,000, through Thundafund’s crowdsourcing platform.

The Colors From Africa Fellowship

The entire 4 weeks incubator experience that begins Tuesday 15th November 2022, will run via online and offline workshops that will culminate in a 3 day Business Exhibition at Jenga Leo offices on December 10-12th 2022. Learn to apply.
